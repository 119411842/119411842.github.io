General Consultancy Information

Hyper automation deals with the application of advanced technologies, including artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), to increasingly automate processes and augment humans.

Multiexperience, is seeing the traditional idea of a computer evolves from a single point of interaction to include multisensory and multitouchpoint interfaces like wearables and advanced computer sensors.

Distributed cloud is the application of cloud computing technologies to interconnect data and applications served from multiple geographic locations.
